关于一地™

一地景观事务所创立于2017年岁末。创始人是在美国的数据工程师郭弋雍和中国的景观设计师刘京一。 美国传统的Single Family House赋予每个家庭建造属于自己庭院的独特机会。以自然与艺术为主导的景观，对人们生活的幸福感有着直接或潜在的影响。 一地的两位创始人相信自然美好的景观构造是空间升华的精髓所在。 拥有“活动性”，“参与感”，“丰富度”的空间景观将会使得房屋的价值与体验得到立体性地提升。 一地致力于将世界级大规模主题性景观设计方案应用于每一个热爱自然与生活的家庭的前院与后院。

因为在一地的眼中，您的一地就是中央公园。

About Yiland™

Yiland studio was founded by Yiyong Guo and Jingyi Liu at the end of 2017. The traditional single family houses in the U.S. offer the possibility to owners to create their own gardens. Landscape empowered by nature and art has direct or subtle impact on people's happiness. The founders of Yiland believe in the force of gorgeous landscape design in making houses inspired. Space with self-esteem, self-identity and diversity will level up the value and experience of houses exponentially. Yiland provides world class and large scale landscape solution to every family who loves nature and life.

In the eyes of Yiland，Your yard is the central park.
